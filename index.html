Enterprise-Grade Security

Patient Privacy is Our Foundation

We process highly sensitive medical and biometric data. Our infrastructure is built from the ground up to exceed international compliance standards.

HIPAA & GDPR Compliant

Our systems are fully compliant with the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act, ensuring strict handling of all Protected Health Information (PHI).

End-to-End Encryption

All audio, motor, and cognitive data is encrypted using AES-256 in transit and at rest. Only authorized clinical providers hold the decryption keys.

Anonymized Processing

Before any data reaches our neural networks, all Personally Identifiable Information (PII) is completely stripped and tokenized.
